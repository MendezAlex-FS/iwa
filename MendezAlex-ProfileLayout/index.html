Gaming

League of Legends (LoL), commonly referred to as League, is a 2009 multiplayer online battle arena video game developed and published by Riot Games. Inspired by Defense of the Ancients, a custom map for Warcraft III, Riot's founders sought to develop a stand-alone game in the same genre. Since its release in October 2009, League has been free-to-play and is monetized through purchasable character customization. The game is available for Microsoft Windows and macOS.

Cooking

Cooking Gordon Ramsay’s lobster risotto is one of my favorite experiences. From preparing the lobster to achieving the perfect creamy risotto, the process is incredibly rewarding. The rich, buttery lobster combined with the flavors from the broth, wine, and parmesan creates a comforting yet elegant dish. I love the precision and patience required, especially when slowly stirring the rice to release its starch. It’s a dish that demands attention and technique, making it even more satisfying when it turns out perfectly.
