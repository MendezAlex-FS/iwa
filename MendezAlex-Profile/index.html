Traveling

Traveling has given me the opportunity to experience diverse cultures, landscapes, and histories, shaping my perspective in ways I never imagined. From the tropical beauty of Hawaii to the bustling streets of New York and LA, each place has left its mark on me. Washington DC and Virginia provided a deep dive into American history, while states like Kentucky and Georgia gave me a taste of the South’s rich traditions and hospitality. Whether exploring the deserts of Arizona or the coastal beauty of the Carolinas, each journey has broadened my appreciation for the different ways people live across the U.S.

Beyond the United States, my travels have taken me to incredible destinations like South Africa and Germany, where I was able to experience vastly different cultures and histories. South Africa’s vibrant energy and stunning landscapes were unforgettable, and I found its complex history both eye-opening and humbling. Germany, with its deep-rooted traditions and modern innovation, gave me a fascinating contrast of old and new. Walking through historic cities, learning about different cultures firsthand, and engaging with people from different backgrounds made these trips incredibly enriching.

Through all these travels, I’ve gained more than just memories—I’ve developed a greater sense of curiosity, adaptability, and understanding of the world. Every destination has offered a unique lesson, whether in the form of history, culture, or the simple joy of experiencing something new. Travel has reinforced my appreciation for diversity and deepened my desire to continue exploring new places, meeting new people, and learning from the world around me.

Technology

What I love about technology is the endless creativity it allows, whether I’m developing a website or building a computer. It never feels monotonous—there’s always a new challenge to tackle, a new problem to solve, or a new way to optimize something. Every project feels like a unique puzzle, where I get to piece things together in a way that best fits the needs of the task at hand. In web development, no two sites are exactly alike; even with similar frameworks or designs, there are always nuances that make each one distinct. The process of coding, debugging, and refining ensures that there’s always something new to learn, keeping the experience fresh and exciting.

Building PCs, in particular, is something that fascinates me because no two builds are ever truly identical. Even when using the same components, small variations in factors like thermal performance, power efficiency, or benchmarking results make each build a unique experience. The precision involved in assembling parts, optimizing airflow, and fine-tuning performance adds an element of craftsmanship that keeps me engaged. It’s a blend of science and artistry, where technical knowledge meets hands-on creativity. That unpredictability—where even a slight variation in assembly can change the outcome—makes every build an exciting and rewarding endeavor.

Gaming

League of Legends (LoL), commonly referred to as League, is a 2009 multiplayer online battle arena video game developed and published by Riot Games. Inspired by Defense of the Ancients, a custom map for Warcraft III, Riot's founders sought to develop a stand-alone game in the same genre. Since its release in October 2009, League has been free-to-play and is monetized through purchasable character customization. The game is available for Microsoft Windows and macOS.

Cooking

Cooking Gordon Ramsay’s lobster risotto is one of my favorite culinary experiences. There’s something incredibly rewarding about the process—from carefully preparing the lobster to achieving the perfect creamy texture of the risotto. The balance of rich, buttery lobster with the depth of flavors from the broth, wine, and parmesan creates an elegant yet comforting dish. I enjoy the precision it requires, the patience needed to slowly stir the rice to release its starch, and the way each ingredient plays a role in building layers of flavor. It’s a dish that demands attention and technique, which makes it all the more satisfying when it turns out perfectly.

Beyond just the taste, I love making lobster risotto because it feels like an elevated experience every time. It’s not just a meal; it’s a process that engages all the senses—the aroma of shallots and garlic sautéing, the rich seafood-infused broth simmering, and the vibrant colors of the finished dish. Whether I’m cooking it for myself as a treat or sharing it with others, it always feels special. There’s something about perfecting a dish crafted by a world-renowned chef like Gordon Ramsay that makes it even more enjoyable, pushing me to refine my cooking skills with every attempt.

Fishing

Fishing is more than just a hobby for me—it’s a way to connect with myself and the world around me. There’s something incredibly peaceful about being near the water, feeling the rhythm of the waves, and waiting patiently for a bite. The stillness and anticipation create a meditative experience where my mind, body, and soul align. In those moments, nothing else exists; the distractions of daily life fade away, leaving only the simple joy of being present. Whether I’m fishing alone or with others, it’s always a grounding experience that brings me a deep sense of calm and clarity.

Beyond the tranquility, fishing also gives me a sense of challenge and reward. The patience required, the strategy behind choosing the right bait, and the thrill of finally feeling a tug on the line make every trip exciting. No two fishing experiences are the same—sometimes it’s about the quiet appreciation of nature, while other times, it’s about the adrenaline of reeling in a big catch. Regardless of the outcome, fishing always leaves me refreshed, reminding me to slow down, appreciate the little things, and enjoy the connection between nature and myself.
